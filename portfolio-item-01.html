QuarantineSupportApplication

Provides symptom analysis and stress relief

What was our inspiration?

The project was inspired by the pandemic, and actually a personal story from one of our team members, where a family friend was unsure of his next course of action regarding his symptoms, and anxiety regarding what to do. We realized that many people may have undue stress, and from this, the project sprung forth.

What functions does our project provide?

Our app provides 5 different functions which include user login with OTP verification, a settings page which allows users to change their personal information, a matching game to interact with other users, a guided breathing exercise that can be used to relieve stress, and finally a symptom assessment checker.

The picture above shows one of the main funtions of the project which is a symptom assessment of COVID and determines its presence. If app reccomends doctor visit, user can enter their address and preferred travel distance/mobility, and the app will compile list of hospitals to visit within travel distance, in order. They can also be accessed through google maps.

What tools/languages did we use?

We built this application using Javafx GUI and javax mail library. TomTom API and mapquest were used for retrieving hospital locations. Image/Videos were made using Canva, Audacity, Shortcut
